About

This site hosts the ancestry research and stories for the family at Abbey Terrace. It is published from the family-book tooling.

Currently featuring:

  • Interactive full family tree visualizer (force-directed graph of 1200+ people)
  • Stories from the family book (Thomson, Bailey, Wharton, Boyle lines and more)

Built with ❤️ using family-book tools. Source in family-book repo. Published to ancestry.abbey-terrace.uk via GitHub Pages.
